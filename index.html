About the event!

EVO TEKKEN 8 - is the biggest 3D fighting tournament where you will be able to see an incredible show of amazing talents that will all be competing for the grand prize
Where Location: Venue: Mandalay Bay Convention Center City: Las Vegas, Nevada, USA Dates: July 19–21, 2024
How to Join Competitors: Register online via the EVO official website. Early bird discounts are available, so sign up quickly! Spectators: Tickets for live audiences are on sale. Choose from general admission, premium seating, or VIP packages. Online: Stream all matches live on Twitch and YouTube.
Organizing Committee EVO 2024 is brought to you by the Evolution Championship Series Team, supported by industry leaders, including Bandai Namco, Capcom, and the global fighting game community. The committee is dedicated to ensuring a seamless, inclusive, and competitive experience for all attendees.
